ABOUT

This website is dedicated to Matt Hellards collection of chilled alternative acoustic songs and what he's up to.

Matt is not your conventional guitarist, with the use of percussion, alternate tunings, partial capos, two handed techniques, harmonica and banjo and his very personal song-writing style, he allows us, the listeners, to feel as if you are taking the journey with him. Although these techniques have been seen before, Matt manages to eke out something new, where the genre is ever changing from song to song. Involving traditional folk, rock, and even occasionally reggae themes, all with an underlying feel of relaxation.
